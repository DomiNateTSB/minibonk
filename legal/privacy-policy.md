## 2. Data I Collect

### 2.1 Account Data
When you register an account, I collect:
- **Email address** — used for authentication, account recovery, and service communications
- **Display name / username** — used to identify you in the game and on leaderboards
- **Password** — stored as a secure cryptographic hash by Supabase; I never have access to your plaintext password
- **Account creation date and time**

### 2.2 Gameplay Data
During your use of the Service, I collect and store:
- **Game progress:** soul count, purchased upgrades, best wave reached, best kill count, best level reached
- **Statistics:** total kills, total runs, total souls earned, total boss kills, best performance per run type
- **Run history:** the last 5 runs including class played, wave reached, kills, level, and date
- **Battle Pass data:** current level, XP, and which rewards have been claimed
- **Achievements unlocked**
- **Inventory:** which in-game items you own and in what quantity
- **Daily Challenge records:** your score and completion status per day

### 2.3 Payment Data
When you purchase the Battle Pass or Battle Pass Premium:
- I receive confirmation of whether your payment was successful, the product tier purchased, and your user ID — but **I never see or store your payment card details**
- All payment card data is processed exclusively by **Stripe, Inc.** and is subject to their privacy policy

### 2.4 Technical and Log Data
The following data may be collected automatically:
- **IP address** — collected by our hosting provider (Vercel) for security and abuse prevention purposes
- **Browser type and version**
- **Device type**
- **Pages visited and interactions** (via Vercel's infrastructure logs)
- **Error logs** — technical errors that occur during gameplay

### 2.5 Data I Do Not Collect
I do not collect:
- Precise geolocation data
- Microphone or camera data
- Contacts or address book data
- Any data from minors under 13 years of age (if I become aware of such data, I will delete it immediately)

## 9. Data Security

I implement appropriate technical and organizational security measures to protect your personal data, including:

- **Encryption in transit:** All data is transmitted over HTTPS/TLS
- **Authentication security:** Passwords are hashed using industry-standard algorithms by Supabase; I never see plaintext passwords
- **Row-Level Security (RLS):** Database access controls ensure each user can only access their own data
- **Access controls:** Administrative database access is restricted and protected
- **Payment security:** Payment card data is handled exclusively by Stripe (PCI DSS compliant) and never passes through my systems

Despite these measures, no method of transmission over the internet or electronic storage is 100% secure. I cannot guarantee absolute security of your data.

If I become aware of a data breach that affects your rights and freedoms, I will notify the relevant supervisory authority within 72 hours and inform affected users without undue delay, as required by GDPR.

## 10. Cookies and Local Storage

Thornrift uses **browser localStorage** (not cookies) to store session preferences and cached game data locally on your device. This data includes:

- Cached game progress (as a local backup of server data)
- Audio/visual settings

This local data is not transmitted to third parties and remains on your device. You can clear it at any time through your browser settings.

Vercel and Supabase may use technical cookies necessary for the operation of their infrastructure. These are strictly necessary cookies and do not require consent under the ePrivacy Directive.
