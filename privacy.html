Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 25, 2026  ·  GDPR compliant

1. Who We Are

Thornrift is operated by Daniel, a private individual. Data controller contact: daniel@kodutankod.se

2. Data We Collect

Account data: Email address, display name, and hashed password (I never see your plaintext password).

Gameplay data: Game progress (souls, upgrades, best scores), run statistics, run history (last 5 runs), Battle Pass level and XP, achievements, inventory, and Daily Challenge records.

Payment data: Only purchase confirmation and tier — your card details go exclusively to Stripe and never pass through our systems.

Technical data: IP address (via Vercel infrastructure), browser type, and error logs.

We do not collect: Location, microphone/camera data, contacts, or data from children under 13.

3. Legal Basis (GDPR)

We process your data under: Contract (Art. 6(1)(b)) for account and gameplay data; Legitimate interests (Art. 6(1)(f)) for leaderboards and security; Legal obligation (Art. 6(1)(c)) for financial record-keeping.

4. How We Use Your Data

To provide the Service, authenticate your account, save progress, display leaderboards, manage the Battle Pass, detect cheating and fraud, and improve the game. We do not use your data for advertising and we never sell your data.

5. Data Sharing

We share data only with these service providers:

  • Supabase — authentication and database (EU hosting) — privacy policy
  • Stripe, Inc. — payment processing (EU-US Data Privacy Framework) — privacy policy
  • Vercel, Inc. — web hosting (SCCs in place) — privacy policy

We do not share your data with any other parties except where required by law.

6. Public Leaderboard

Your username and scores are displayed publicly on the leaderboard. Your email address is never shown publicly. To remove your leaderboard entry, contact daniel@kodutankod.se.

7. Data Retention

  • Account and gameplay data: until account deletion
  • Battle Pass ownership records: until account deletion
  • Payment records: 7 years (Swedish accounting law)
  • Server logs: up to 30 days (managed by Vercel/Supabase)

Upon account deletion, personal data is deleted or anonymized within 30 days, except where retention is legally required.

8. Your Rights (GDPR)

If you are in the EEA, you have the right to: access your data, rectify inaccurate data, erasure ("right to be forgotten"), restrict processing, data portability (JSON format available on request), object to processing, and withdraw consent at any time.

Exercise any right by contacting daniel@kodutankod.se. We respond within 30 days.

You may also lodge a complaint with the Swedish data protection authority: Integritetsskyddsmyndigheten (IMY) at imy.se.

9. Security

We use HTTPS/TLS for all data in transit, cryptographic password hashing, Row-Level Security on the database, and PCI DSS-compliant payment processing via Stripe. In the event of a breach affecting your rights, we will notify the relevant authority within 72 hours and inform affected users promptly.

10. Local Storage

Thornrift uses browser localStorage (not cookies) to store cached game data and preferences locally on your device. This data is not shared with third parties. You can clear it at any time through your browser settings.

11. Children

The Service is not directed at children under 13. If we learn we have collected data from a child under 13, we will delete it immediately. Contact daniel@kodutankod.se if you believe this has occurred.

12. Changes

We may update this policy and will note the date of changes above. Continued use constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
